# Metadata – Glass House

**Item ID:** item4  
**Title:** Glass House  
**Creator:** Philip Johnson  
**Year:** 1949  
**Location:** New Canaan, Connecticut  
**Description:** A transparent pavilion built almost entirely with glass walls and a thin steel frame. The Glass House explores openness, minimal structure, and interaction with landscape. It is a key work of American modernism.
