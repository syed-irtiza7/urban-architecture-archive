# Case Study House No. 22 (Stahl House)

**Architect:** Pierre Koenig  
**Year:** 1960  
**Location:** Hollywood Hills, Los Angeles, California

Case Study House No. 22 is one of the most iconic examples of mid-century modern residential architecture. Designed as part of the Case Study House program, it emphasizes glass, steel, and open-plan living. The house features floor-to-ceiling windows that overlook the city, representing the postwar shift toward light, transparency, and modern material use.
