# Villa Savoye

**Architect:** Le Corbusier  
**Year:** 1931  
**Location:** Poissy, France

Villa Savoye represents the peak of Le Corbusier’s “Five Points of Architecture,” incorporating ribbon windows, pilotis, open floor plans, and rooftop gardens. It remains a central work of the International Style and modern architecture.
