# Eames House (Case Study House No. 8)

**Architects:** Charles and Ray Eames  
**Year:** 1949  
**Location:** Pacific Palisades, California

The Eames House is a modular structure composed of prefabricated steel parts and brightly colored exterior panels. It demonstrates efficient construction methods and an artistic residential aesthetic, making it a landmark example of postwar domestic modernism.

This item includes descriptive content and images that support its preservation in digital format.
