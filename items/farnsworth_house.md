# Farnsworth House

**Architect:** Ludwig Mies van der Rohe  
**Year:** 1951  
**Location:** Plano, Illinois

The Farnsworth House is a masterpiece of minimalist modernism. Built using steel and expansive glass panels, it embodies Mies’s philosophy of “less is more.” Elevated above ground to protect against flooding, its simplicity, structural clarity, and open interior influenced generations of modern architects.
